Smirnoff - Dance Exchange

Nightlife Exchange

Smirnoff's Nightlife Exchange is a global effort to show the world how other cultures party. The Dance Exchange was an effort to share how people dance. Our friends Aaron Meyers and Zach Lieberman had already created software to show stunning visualizations of dances and capture videos of dances in "dance booths". It was our job to create a solution to share it with the world.

One Night Only, GIF It Your All

Under The Hood

Not only did we create a web service to convert movies to animated gifs on the fly, but we also crafted a tiny API for Aaron and Zach to use so parties around the world could see dances as they were happening. The result of which was a real-time study in partying around the globe.
